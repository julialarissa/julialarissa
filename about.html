Hi! My name is Julia Larissa Geisler. I'm originally from a small town in Vemont, USA not unlike Stars Hollow in Gilmore Girls! I'm currently pursuing a Master of Information focused in UX Design at the University of Toronto. At present, I'm situated just outside New York City while waiting out the pandemic.

My undergraduate background is in marketing, and when I heard about UX Design, I realized it sounded like marketing turned on its head. Rather than targeting a user with a set product, I would be taking that product and updating it to the user's needs. I hope to better understand how UX Design can serve the service industries in a rapidly changing technological environment, and to create a user-focused experience that addresses the users' pain points.

No longer can companies simply conceptualize a product and put it on the market with a swanky marketing deck, they must take their customers’ needs into account from the initial ideation and keep updating based on continued research to stay competitive.
