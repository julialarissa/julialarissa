Hi! My name is Julia Larissa Geisler. I'm originally from a small town in Vermont, USA not unlike Stars Hollow in Gilmore Girls! I'm currently pursuing a Master of Information focused in UX Design at the University of Toronto. At present, I'm situated just outside New York City while waiting out the pandemic.

I aim to understand how UX Design can better serve the service industries in a rapidly changing technological environment, and to create a user-focused experience that addresses the users' pain points. No longer can companies simply conceptualize a product and put it on the market with a swanky marketing deck, they must take their customers’ needs into account from the initial ideation and keep updating based on continued research to stay competitive.
