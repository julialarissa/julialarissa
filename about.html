Hi! My name is Julia Larissa Geisler. I'm currently pursuing a Master of Information focused in UX Design at the University of Toronto. At present, I'm situated just outside New York City.

After studying marketing in undergraduate and getting to work on the flows of websites and apps at Vinous, I became interested in just how users are affected by the products we create. This led me to UX Design. I love trying to solve the challenges presented by improving on or creating products not only based on brilliant ideas, but also based on how a user experiences these products.

I aim to understand how UX Design can better support companies in a rapidly changing technological environment, and to create a user-focused experience that addresses the users' pain points to build a healthier relationship between the user and product. No longer can companies simply conceptualize a product and put it on the market with a swanky marketing deck, they must take their customers’ needs into account from the initial ideation and frequently update based on continued research to stay competitive.
